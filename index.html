About John Le

Delivering Engineered Solutions!

With over 13 years of experience in the field of manufacturing, fabrication, installation and construction of pre-engineered steel structures, we are among the long-standing reputable names in the Vietnamese steel industry that have earned the love and trust of many domestic and foreign customers.

Our aim is to become a supplier of carbon steel and specialty steel products, including pre-fabricated frames that provide hassle-free and fast customer service. To achieve our standard of excellence, we are committed to work on the following values:
